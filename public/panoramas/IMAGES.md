## Format

- **Type:** JPEG or WebP
- **Aspect ratio:** 2:1 (equirectangular)
- **Recommended size:** 4096×2048 px minimum for good quality
- **Max size:** Keep under 8 MB per image for fast loading

Images are referenced in `data/places.json` as `/panoramas/<filename>`.
